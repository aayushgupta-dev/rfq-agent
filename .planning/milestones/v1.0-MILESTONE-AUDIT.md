### Findings (both WARNING, non-blocking)

- **WARNING-1 — `/data/rfq` regenerate is a user-visible no-op (DATA-04, UI-01).** `regen-button.tsx` fetches the live regen then reloads, but `rfq/page.tsx` renders a build-time static import. Regenerated RFQ is fetched and discarded; the buyer sees the same committed RFQ. Downstream screens also ground against the static RFQ by design. *Rubric note:* DATA-04 ("regenerable live in-app") and the §24 "dynamic, never hardcoded" principle are fully met for extraction & comparison, but the RFQ screen's regen is non-functional for the buyer.
- **WARNING-2 — `vendor_fluff` fixture drift (DATA-01/02, trace fidelity).** Source `data/vendor_fluff.json` and served `apps/web/public/data/vendor_fluff.json` differ in `raw_text`/`mess_spec`. No wiring break, but the committed `trace_vendor_fluff.json` was captured from the source copy → the live demo "fluff" extraction won't be byte-reproducible against the committed trace.
