**Key accomplishments:**

- **Grounding Gate & Messy Data** — An LLM-free grounding gate that validates every evidence span against source text (recomputes offsets, downgrades fabrications to `unsupported`, never trusts a model "verified" flag), plus 1 realistic marketing-services RFQ and 3 deliberately-messy vendor responses generated by prompt and asserted messy in tests.
- **Extraction Agent** — Grounding-gated per-vendor extraction across all 8 categories with evidence snippets, four explicit absence flags (never fills missing info), truncation/refusal treated as hard errors, SSE streaming, and captured pipeline traces.
- **Comparison Agent** — Comparability-before-ranking over code-validated `ExtractionResult[]`: a fail-closed verdict clamp (comparable/partially/not_comparable) that never aggregates over a missing field, qualitative readiness signals instead of a numeric leaderboard, and code-seeded clarification questions.

- **Reliability throughout** — Contradictory grand totals surface as `total_price=conflicting` (both values kept) via a prompt-side fix with a live behavioral test; the grounding gate stays one-directional per CLAUDE.md §8.
