# Request for Quotation (RFQ) - GlowBite 18-Month Go-to-Market Marketing Services Program

**Client:** Luminos Consumer Brands
**Issue Date:** 2026-07-15
**Response Deadline:** 2026-08-12
**Total Budget (USD):** $1,615,000

## Scope Summary

Luminos Consumer Brands invites qualified agencies to submit commercial quotations for the 18-month go-to-market launch program for GlowBite, a children's vitamin gummy range for ages 4-12. The RFQ covers strategy, creative development, TVC development and production, organic social content, paid media planning and buying, children's advertising and claims compliance, and end-to-end launch program management. Work is expected to commence in Q4 2026, with initial launch-market deliverables required ahead of a 12-week campaign window beginning in January 2027. Agencies may bid for one, several, or all line items; however, Luminos reserves the right to award line items to multiple vendors. All proposals must include detailed scope assumptions, itemised third-party costs, timeline commitments, resource plans, risk controls, and explicit compliance capability for child-directed advertising and product-claims substantiation.

## Line Items

### strategy-creative: Strategy & Creative Development

Development of GlowBite launch strategy and creative foundation across both consumer and trade audiences. Scope includes brand narrative, target audience segmentation, shopper and parent insight synthesis, brand positioning, messaging architecture, campaign platform, creative route development, and launch communications framework for retail sell-in and consumer activation.

**Deliverables:**
- Kick-off workshop with Luminos brand, sales, regulatory, and procurement stakeholders
- Audience segmentation framework covering parents/caregivers, gift purchasers, and trade stakeholders
- Brand narrative and positioning statement for GlowBite launch market
- Messaging architecture including mandatory claims language, proof points, and tone-of-voice guardrails
- Trade communications framework for retailer and distributor presentations
- Consumer campaign concept deck with minimum 3 distinct creative routes for review
- Refined preferred creative route after first-round client feedback
- Brand playbook including campaign platform, visual direction, key messages, channel role summary, and do/don't guidance
- Launch tagline and core copy lines for TV, digital video, social, OOH, and trade use
- Final recommendation presentation delivered in editable and PDF formats

**Timeline:** 8 weeks
**Budget Range (USD):** $90,000 – $140,000

Creative development of television commercial assets derived from the approved campaign platform. Scope includes concept adaptation for film, scriptwriting, storyboard development, animatic recommendation if required, director shortlist and treatment briefing, and casting brief preparation. **Deliverables:**

- Frame-by-frame storyboard for each approved film asset
- Optional low-fidelity animatic recommendation and scene timing guide

- Casting brief covering minimum 2 on-screen child roles plus parent/caregiver role requirements
- Shot list and production rationale for handoff to selected production partner
- Final approved script package cleared for compliance review prior to production

**Timeline:** 6 weeks
**Budget Range (USD):** $70,000 – $110,000

## Commercial Expectations

Luminos Consumer Brands expects commercially robust and transparent proposals. Preferred payment structure for each awarded line item is 30% at contract signature, 30% upon completion of an agreed mid-point milestone tied to defined deliverables, and 40% upon final delivery sign-off; payment in full at project commencement will not be accepted. Bidders must specify milestone definitions by line item in their response. All quoted fees must be inclusive of agency profit margin, management fees, and overhead; no undisclosed markups on third-party costs are permitted. Third-party costs must be itemised separately and clearly labelled as pass-through or managed service costs. For paid media buying, all media owner invoices must be passed through to Luminos and any service fee, commission, or optimisation fee must be separately stated. The media buying agency must provide gross-to-net reconciliation within 30 days of each quarterly media flight. Vendors must disclose whether pricing is based on project fee, retainer, day rate, commission, or hybrid model, and must state assumptions on number of revision rounds, included meetings, and out-of-scope triggers. Vendors must hold professional indemnity insurance of not less than USD 2,000,000 per occurrence and provide evidence prior to contract award. Contracted service levels will include milestone dates; a 15% performance penalty applies to the relevant invoice for missed agreed milestones attributable to the vendor, unless delay is caused by documented Luminos approval hold, force majeure, or approved scope change. For TV buying and delivery, agencies must commit to a minimum of 90% of booked GRP delivery and provide make-goods or fee relief for under-delivery. Luminos reserves the right to negotiate final scope, split awards across agencies, request best-and-final-offer pricing, and reject any proposal that does not meet compliance, transparency, or insurance requirements.

## Compliance Requirements

- All creative content targeting children under 13 must comply with COPPA (US) and applicable advertising codes before production. A qualified compliance officer's sign-off is mandatory before shoot.
- Product efficacy and nutritional claims must be substantiated with documented evidence prior to inclusion in any script, visual, or media asset. No implied health claims without regulatory clearance.
- Media agency must provide a signed disclosure of all media owner rebates, volume bonuses, or commercial arrangements that affect the net media rate. Failure to disclose is grounds for contract termination.
- Agency must maintain and make available on request a conflict-of-interest register covering all clients in the children's food/beverage and FMCG categories.
- Data privacy: any digital campaign element targeting users under 13 must be designed for COPPA compliance with written confirmation from the agency's legal or compliance function.
- No TVC, digital video, social content batch, influencer brief, or paid media audience setup may proceed to production or trafficking without documented compliance review and written approval.
- If any UK media is proposed, all applicable child-directed creative and scheduling must be reviewed against CAP/BCAP Code requirements before booking.
- All child talent use must include appropriate guardian consent, usage-rights documentation, and safeguarding procedures appropriate to age and production setting.
- Agencies must retain claims substantiation files, approval logs, and final compliance sign-off records for not less than 24 months after campaign completion and make them available to Luminos on request.
- Any identified compliance breach, data privacy issue, or claim challenge must be reported to Luminos within one business day together with remediation steps and exposure assessment.
